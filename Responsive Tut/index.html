About This Website

This is a website I created to learn how to make responsive websites. Responsive websites are really cool, they change shape to better fit the device you are using to access them. Its like a shape shifting website It is not as hard to do as it seems, its quiet simple actually.

Why make this website?

Making the website is the best and fastest way of getting a good understanding as well as a practice of the topic, it helps in better understanding the process as compared to just reading. To be honest I have no idea what I am writing, I just need to fill some lines to test the website out so I am writing anything that comes to my mind, also its a good typing practice to do every once in a while.
